Daydreamer

Group planning and Event Recommendation

This is a Fullstack web application and capstone project for my bootcamp completion. It is running on node.js Express.js, React-Redux and a host of other npm modules. Users can sign in and create groups, and select interests. The app will compile the group's interests and call on the yelp and eventful API's to search local happenings/venus and recommend a group outing that will hopefully be enjoyable for all group members.

The app also has scheduling capablity thanks to Cronify which will let users authorize their calendars, and will search for free times across all group members making scheduling a breeze.
